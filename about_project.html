What is this project about

This is an idea (one another idea) how to close your tasks in time.

It should be some task-manager or ToDo list, as you wish.

The idea is: connecting this service with special Telegram-bot in your Telegram-messenger.

No other apps in your smartphone, no other downloads on your laptop (or PC).

Just one web-site and one bot in your messenger
